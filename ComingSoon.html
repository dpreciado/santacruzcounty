Arizona Borderlands Democrats

The Arizona Borderlands Democrats is a Democratic Party club in Santa Cruz County, serving primarily the Sonoita-Elgin-Patagonia region. As stated in our bylaws, the purpose of ABD is to support and advance the principles and ideals of the Democratic Party, to provide a regular forum for candidates, and to keep the electorate informed on current events and issues.
